Our Mission

Who We Are

CHASExcellence is a nonprofit scholarship organization founded in honor of Chase Culp #25, a 12-year-old athlete from Colorado Springs whose passion for basketball, fierce dedication, and warm spirit touched everyone around him.

Chase was more than a player — he was a teammate, a leader, and a role model. Our mission is to keep his memory alive by investing in young athletes who reflect the same values Chase brought to the game and to life every single day.

The Foundation

Core Values

Every scholarship recipient is evaluated on the four values Chase embodied.

T

Toughness

Mental and physical strength through adversity, on and off the court.

C

Composure

Staying calm and focused under pressure, leading by example.

U

Unity

Being a great teammate and lifting others up in pursuit of a shared goal.

D

Dedication

Consistent effort, commitment to improvement, and love for the game.

For Athletes

The Scholarship

The CHASExcellence Basketball Scholarship helps cover club team costs and gear for deserving players who represent Chase's values.

🏀

Who Can Apply

Young basketball players who demonstrate Toughness, Composure, Unity, and Dedication in their team and community. Coaches may nominate players on their behalf.

🏆

What's Covered

Scholarship funds go toward club team fees, uniforms, and gear — removing financial barriers so that talent and heart determine who gets to play.

📝

How to Apply

Fill out the scholarship application below. Coaches can also scan the QR code at CHASExcellence events to nominate a deserving player directly.
